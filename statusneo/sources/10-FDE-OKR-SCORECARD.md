# FDE 30 / 60 / 90 — OKR Scorecard
## FDE Practice Activation — Addendum 10 (Tracking Sheet)
*Fill Actual at each horizon close · score every Key Result 0.0–1.0 · a horizon's score is the mean of its KRs · companion to Addendum 09 · June 2026*

**How to use.** This is the companion tracking grid to Addendum 09. The Key Results and Targets are fixed; the three right-hand columns (Actual / Score / R-A-G) are for you to complete. At each 30 / 60 / 90 checkpoint, record the **Actual**, score the KR **0.0–1.0** (the fraction of target achieved — binary KRs score 0 or 1), and mark its status. Average a horizon's KR scores for its headline number. Treat ~0.7 as on-track; a steady 1.0 means the targets were set too low.

**RAG legend:** ● Green — score ≥ 0.70 (on-track) · ● Amber — 0.40–0.69 (at-risk) · ● Red — < 0.40 (off-track).
