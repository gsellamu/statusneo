The gap & the model

The gap: the frameworks (Authentic AI™ Loop, OS Fabric™, AI-Native SDLC) are excellent, but frameworks don't deploy themselves inside a customer's constraints, politics, and regulated data.

FDE lands. GCC scales. One pod, no handoffs.

  • FDE (onshore): embedded, senior, customer-trusted — diagnoses, architects, lands a working slice on the customer's stack.
  • GCC (offshore): velocity + cost — scales delivery behind the trusted relationship.
  • The FDE stays single owner of the outcome and pulls the pod in behind earned trust — no handoff gap.
