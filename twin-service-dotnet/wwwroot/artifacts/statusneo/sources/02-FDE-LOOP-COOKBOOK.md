# The FDE Loop™ — Cookbook
## How a StatusNeo Forward Deployed Engineer Operates
**Companion to:** the FDE Charter Proposal · **Audience:** FDEs, delivery leads, the sponsor

> A cookbook is *methodology* — the repeatable recipes an FDE follows. This maps the embedded-delivery motion onto StatusNeo's existing AuthenticAI™ Loop, so FDE is not a new philosophy — it is the **onsite embodiment** of the one StatusNeo already owns.

---

## THE CORE PRINCIPLE

> **The FDE makes the AuthenticAI™ Loop real at the customer site.**
> Offshore pods run the Loop in the GCC. The FDE runs it *inside the customer*, earns the trust, and pulls the GCC in behind them. One pod, always on, no handoffs.

The four AuthenticAI™ Loop stages — **Clarity → Build → Velocity → Improvement** (Design → Engineer → Automate → Govern) — become the FDE's four operating modes per account.

---

## RECIPE 1 — CLARITY (Design): Diagnose with the customer, not for them
**Goal:** earn the right to architect by understanding the customer's real problem better than they've articulated it.

**Ingredients:** the customer's existing system, their stated roadmap, their actual blockers.

**Method:**
1. **Study their platform before their job description.** Read their architecture, their docs, their constraints. Diagnose where the product "lives between the tools."
2. **Find the load-bearing gap** — the one missing capability that blocks production/paying customers. Name it in their language.
3. **Map it to the OS Fabric™** — which of the six fabrics (Developer, Data, Ops, Security, Experience, Decision) addresses it.
4. **Validate the diagnosis live** — "here's what I see; tell me where I'm wrong." Curiosity, not correction.

**Done when:** the customer agrees on the problem and wants your architecture. *(This is exactly how the Modutecture engagement opened — diagnose "validation has no home," validate live, earn the demo.)*

---

## RECIPE 2 — BUILD (Engineer): Land a working vertical slice on their stack
**Goal:** prove the architecture with running code inside their environment — not a deck.

**Method:**
1. **Build the thinnest end-to-end slice** that proves the thesis, on the customer's actual stack.
2. **Governed-AI discipline** — the model proposes, a deterministic gate disposes. Never blur this in regulated domains. This is AuthenticAI™ "responsible, not reckless" in code.
3. **Label built / proven / next** — never overclaim. The honesty *is* the senior signal and the AuthenticAI™ brand.
4. **Make it observable** — health, metrics, governance from day one (the Govern fabric, embedded early).

**Done when:** a working, customer-validated slice runs on their stack and proves the architecture.

---

## RECIPE 3 — VELOCITY (Automate): Pull the offshore pod in behind earned trust
**Goal:** scale delivery by bringing StatusNeo's GCC velocity in *after* the relationship is de-risked.

**Method:**
1. **Define the offshore-pull point** — once the slice is trusted, scope what the offshore pod takes (breadth, repetition, automation).
2. **Bring the Agents Library + AI-Native SDLC** — agentic CI/CD, autonomous QA, the StatusNeo automation IP.
3. **FDE stays the single owner** — the offshore pod scales delivery; the FDE owns the customer outcome and architecture authority. No handoff gap.
4. **Instrument the flywheel** — correction deltas, velocity metrics, so improvement compounds.

**Done when:** offshore velocity is landing behind the FDE, and the account is scaling without a trust gap.

---

## RECIPE 4 — IMPROVEMENT (Govern): Measure, govern, and expand
**Goal:** make the account durable, governed, and growing — land becomes expand.

**Method:**
1. **Govern continuously** — observability, compliance, SLO/SLA loops (the Govern fabric + Maturity Index).
2. **Measure time-to-trust and expansion** — the FDE practice's core metrics.
3. **Expand the footprint** — one landed outcome becomes the next; the FDE opens adjacent problems the GCC scales.
4. **Feed the practice** — every engagement sharpens the FDE Loop™ and the Landing Runbook.

**Done when:** the account is self-sustaining, governed, expanding, and referenceable.

---

## THE FDE ARCHETYPE — who runs these recipes
- **Senior, onshore, architecture-capable** — can diagnose and architect, not just execute.
- **Customer-facing** — earns trust in the room; translates ambiguity to a plan.
- **Regulated-domain fluent** — especially for healthcare/BFSI/defense accounts.
- **AI-native** — governed agentic AI is their default, not an add-on.
- **Low-ego, outcome-owning** — humble about the customer's build, certain about the diagnosis.

> One FDE + one offshore pod = one account, landed and scaling. That is the unit of the practice.
