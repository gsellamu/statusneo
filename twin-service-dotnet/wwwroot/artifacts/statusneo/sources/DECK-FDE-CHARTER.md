## Slide 5 — The FDE Operating Model
**FDE lands. GCC scales. One pod, no handoffs.**

- **FDE — onshore:** embedded, senior, customer-trusted. Runs the AI Maturity Scan, diagnoses, architects, and lands a working slice on the customer's stack.
- **The seam:** the FDE stays single owner of the outcome. Pulls the offshore pod in behind earned trust — no handoff gap.
- **GCC — offshore:** velocity + cost. Scales delivery behind the trusted relationship using the Agents Library + AI-Native SDLC.

*FDE = onshore trust + architecture authority. GCC = offshore velocity + cost. Two halves of one motion.*
