What the documentation does not settle — Pending Tech Clarifications

These are the architecture questions the onboarding material leaves open. The first two are decisive — they determine the size of the rescue. To be confirmed Day 1.

1 · Geometry & state ownership CRITICAL

Where does the authoritative Moducule/room geometry definition live — encapsulated in Unity prefabs/scenes, or in the backend? Decisive test: can a non-Unity client reconstruct a Moducule from backend data alone? This sizes step 0.

2 · Rules authority CRITICAL

Moducules “contain predefined rules and behaviors” (Unity-side) and ASP/SPA validates standards in Databricks (batch). Are rules split across Unity behaviors + the batch pipeline, with no single authority? Where is each rule actually enforced?

3 · Compliance timing

Is standards validation batch-only (Databricks notebooks), or is any check done inline at design time? How and when do validation results surface back to the Unity user?

4 · Unity ↔ backend sync

Is the exchange real-time bidirectional, request/response, or save/load/export? Is any live state synchronized, or is the client authoritative between saves?

5 · Digital-twin representation

Is the “digital twin” a distinct, persisted, versioned model (which store? what schema?), or is it effectively the Unity scene plus DB rows? Is it change-journaled?

6 · Service topology

Monolith or microservices? How many backend services, and where do Moducule rules execute at runtime? (§18 describes layers, not services.)

7 · Eventing / async

Is there any message bus or event streaming, or is the system purely request/response + batch? No async backbone is documented.

8 · Scale & performance

Caching strategy, horizontal scale, concurrency model, multi-region? Not addressed in the documentation.

9 · Migration state

GraphQL → Lakebase/Postgres: how far along, dual-write or cutover, and which store is the source of truth during the migration?

10 · Multitenancy model

AAA + “multitenant access enforcement” are stated — but is isolation enforced at the schema, row, or database level?
