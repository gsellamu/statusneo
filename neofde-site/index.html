The operating model

FDE lands. GCC scales. One pod, no handoffs.

FDE — onshore

  • Embedded, senior, customer-trusted.
  • Runs the AI Maturity Scan, diagnoses, architects, and lands a working slice on the customer's stack.
  • Stays single owner of the outcome — pulls the pod in behind earned trust, no handoff gap.

GCC — offshore

  • Velocity + cost.
  • Scales delivery behind the trusted relationship using the Agents Library + AI-Native SDLC.
  • FDE = onshore trust + architecture authority. GCC = offshore velocity + cost. Two halves of one motion.

It starts with an AI Engineering Maturity Scan

Structured interviews across engineering, platform, DevOps/SRE, and product; a maturity score and capability heatmap; and an opinionated 90-day / 6-month / 12-month roadmap that says where AI creates disproportionate ROI — and where AI should not be used. Then, unlike an assessor who leaves a deck, the FDE ships the fix.
